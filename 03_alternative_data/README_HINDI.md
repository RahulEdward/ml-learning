## The Alternative Data Revolution

ke liye algorithmic trading, new data sources offer an informational advantage if they provide access to information unavailable from traditional sources, or provide access sooner. Following global trends, the investment industry hai rapidly expanding beyond market aur fundamental data to alternative sources to reap alpha through an informational edge. Annual spending on data, technological capabilities, aur related talent hain expected to increase from the current $3 billion by 12.8% annually through 2020.

Today, investors can access macro or company-specific data mein real-time that historically has been available only at a much lower frequency. Use cases ke liye new data sources include the following:
- Online price data on a representative set ka goods aur services can be used to measure inflation
- The number ka store visits or purchases permits real-time estimates ka company or industry-specific sales or economic activity
- Satellite images can reveal agricultural yields, or activity at mines or on oil rigs before this information hai available elsewhere

## Sources ka alternative data

Alternative datasets hain generated by many sources but can be classified at a high level as predominantly produced by:
- Individuals who post on social media, review products, or use search engines
- Businesses that record commercial transactions, mein particular, credit card payments, or capture supply-chain activity as intermediaries
- Sensors that, among many other things, capture economic activity through images such as satellites or security cameras, or through movement patterns such as cell phone towers

The nature ka alternative data continues to evolve rapidly as new data sources become available aur sources previously labeled “alternative” become part ka the mainstream. The Baltic Dry Index (BDI), ke liye instance, assembles data from several hundred shipping companies to approximate the supply/demand ka dry bulk carriers aur hai now available on the Bloomberg Terminal.

Alternative data sources differ mein crucial respects that determine their value or signal content ke liye algorithmic trading strategies.

## Criteria ke liye evaluating alternative datasets

The ultimate objective ka alternative data hai to provide an informational advantage mein the competitive search ke liye trading signals that produce alpha, namely positive, uncorrelated investment returns. mein practice, the signals extracted from alternative datasets can be used on a standalone basis or combined ke saath other signals as part ka a quantitative strategy.
